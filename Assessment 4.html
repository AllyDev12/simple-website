About Us




Stellaris University

Stellaris University is a prominent university committed to promoting global citizenship, innovation, and academic success. Embedded in a vibrant campus environment, our university is dedicated to providing a life-changing educational experience that gives students the values, information, and abilities they need to prosper in a world that is changing quickly.



Academic Programs



Engineering & Technology:


Discover the innovative fields of engineering and technology, where creativity and practical application collide. Our courses in computer science, electrical engineering, and civil engineering provide students the tools they need to address problems in a variety of sectors and influence technology embarking future.

Courses Offered:
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Computer Science
  • Civil Engineering


Business & Management:


With our business and management courses, you may unleash your entrepreneurial drive and potential as a leader. Whether you specialize in business administration, marketing, or entrepreneurship, you'll learn critical skills for navigating the business world's shifting landscape and generating success.

Courses Offered:
  • Business Administration
  • Marketing
  • Entrepreneurship


Health Sciences:


With the help of our Health Sciences programs, we set out on a mission to improve healthcare. You will be prepared to make a meaningful impact on people's lives and communities, from the complex area of medicine to the sensitive care of nursing and the wider views of public health.

Courses Offered:
  • Medicine
  • Sociology
  • Public Health


Humanities & Social Sciences:


Explore the world of concepts and human existence with our courses in the humanities and social sciences. Investigate the workings of society via sociology; examine the intricacies of the human mind through psychology; and learn about the dynamics of government and society through political science.

Courses Offered:
  • Psychology
  • Nursing
  • Political Science
